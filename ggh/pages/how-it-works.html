How Campuslink Works

A simple, safe, and transparent process for finding and connecting with campus service providers.

For Students & Users

Finding a Vendor in 5 Steps

From discovery to service completion — entirely on your terms.

01

Create Free Account

Register with your school email, verify your phone via OTP, and complete your student profile in minutes. Browsing is always free.

02

Search & Filter

Browse by category, search by name or service, filter by rating and plan type to find the perfect verified vendor for your need.

03

Contact Directly

Use the Call or WhatsApp buttons to reach the vendor externally. Agree on terms, price, and delivery timeline — all outside the platform.

04

Transact Offline

Complete your service and payment directly with the vendor. Campuslink is not involved in this step in any way.

05

Leave a Review

Rate your experience (1–5 stars) and write a review to help other students make better decisions. Reviews are moderated before publication.

Important Disclaimer: Campuslink is a digital directory service only. We do not process payments between users and vendors, provide services directly, handle disputes about service delivery, or operate any internal messaging system. All business communication and transactions must occur directly and externally between users and vendors.

For Service Providers

Getting Your Business Listed

A straightforward 5-step process to get your business in front of thousands of students.

01

Choose Vendor Type

Select Student Vendor (enrolled student) or Community Vendor (established business). Different verification requirements apply to each.

02

Fill Your Profile

Enter your personal details, business name, service category, description, price range, and operating location for your listing.

03

Upload Documents

Upload your student ID/CAC certificate, government ID, selfie with ID, and active service photos for identity verification.

04

Pay & Subscribe

Choose Basic, Premium, or Featured plan. Pay securely via Paystack. Server-side verification confirms every payment before activation.

05

Admin Approval

Our admin team reviews your application within 24–48 hours. Once approved, your listing goes live and becomes visible to all students.

Platform Rules

What Campuslink Does & Doesn't Do

We DO

  • Verify and approve vendor identity documents
  • Display verified vendor listings with ratings
  • Moderate and publish student reviews
  • Investigate and act on vendor complaints
  • Process vendor subscription payments securely
  • Suspend or ban vendors who violate our policies

We DON'T

  • Provide or deliver any services to users
  • Process payments between users and vendors
  • Operate internal messaging or chat
  • Guarantee vendor service quality or delivery
  • Act as an agent, employer, or partner of any vendor
  • Mediate or resolve service delivery disputes
