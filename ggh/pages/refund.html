Refund Policy

Last updated: March 2025

Contents

1. General Policy 2. Eligible Refunds 3. Non-Refundable 4. Refund Process 5. Refund Timeline 6. Chargebacks 7. Contact

Note: This policy applies to Campuslink vendor subscription fees only. It does not govern service payments between users and vendors — those are handled entirely between the two parties outside our platform.

1. General Policy

Campuslink subscription fees are payments for directory listing access — not for vendor services delivered to students. Subscription fees are generally non-refundable once a vendor's application has been submitted and payment confirmed, because our platform incurs costs for the identity verification process, admin review time, and listing infrastructure from the moment of payment.

We evaluate refund requests on a case-by-case basis and reserve the right to approve or deny requests at our sole discretion based on the circumstances outlined below.

2. Eligible Refund Situations

Refunds may be issued at Campuslink's sole discretion in the following limited circumstances:

  • Verified Duplicate Payment: Confirmed double-charging for the same subscription period, verified through our Paystack payment records. Full refund of the duplicate charge.
  • Application Rejected — Platform Error: If a vendor application is rejected solely due to a Campuslink system error (not a policy violation or document issue), and payment was collected, a full refund will be processed within 7 business days.
  • Payment Collected Without Activation: If a payment was confirmed by Paystack but no subscription was created due to a technical failure on our end, a full refund will be issued promptly.
  • Admin-Initiated Suspension (Non-Violation): If Campuslink suspends a fully verified, compliant vendor's account due to a platform error or administrative mistake, a pro-rated refund of the unused subscription period may be considered.

3. Non-Refundable Situations

Refunds will not be issued in the following cases:

  • Change of mind after submitting your application or after your listing has been activated.
  • Account suspended, rejected, or deactivated due to policy violations, false information, forged documents, or conduct violations.
  • Failure to generate sufficient revenue, inquiries, or customers through the platform.
  • Dissatisfaction with the number of competitors or categories on the platform.
  • Subscription auto-deactivation due to failure to renew after the grace period.
  • Downgrade requests — downgrades take effect at the end of the current subscription period and no partial refund is issued for the pricing difference.
  • Vendor account suspended following verified complaints from users.

4. Refund Request Process

To request a refund, send an email to billing@campuslink.ng with the following information:

  • Your registered email address
  • Your Paystack payment reference number
  • A clear, factual description of the issue and why you believe a refund is warranted
  • Any supporting documentation (screenshots, error messages, etc.)

Our billing team will acknowledge your request within 48 hours and communicate a decision within 5 business days.

5. Refund Timeline

Approved refunds are processed back to the original payment method via Paystack within 5–10 business days of approval. Actual credit to your bank account or card may take an additional 3–5 business days depending on your financial institution.

6. Chargebacks

Initiating a fraudulent or unwarranted chargeback through your bank, card issuer, or payment platform will result in the immediate and permanent suspension of your Campuslink vendor account and all associated accounts. We maintain complete, cryptographically signed payment records for all transactions and will contest any fraudulent chargeback with Paystack and the relevant financial institution. We may also pursue legal remedies for damages caused by fraudulent chargebacks.

If you have a genuine billing concern, please contact us directly at billing@campuslink.ng before initiating any dispute with your bank.
