Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us. Campuslink is committed to protecting your personal data in accordance with the Nigeria Data Protection Regulation (NDPR) and international best practices.

1. Data We Collect

Campuslink collects the following categories of personal data:

  • Account Data: Full name, school email, personal email, phone number, department, and academic level.
  • Vendor Data: Matric number, business details, uploaded verification documents (student ID, selfie, CAC certificate, government ID, service photos, logo).
  • Usage Data: Login timestamps, IP addresses, browser/device information (user agent), search queries, page visits, and profile interactions.
  • Transaction Data: Paystack payment references, subscription plan details, and payment history. We do not store card numbers, CVV codes, or bank account details.
  • Content Data: Reviews you write, complaints you submit, notifications you receive, and vendors you save.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • Account creation, authentication, and secure session management.
  • Vendor identity verification and admin approval processes.
  • Processing and verifying subscription payments via Paystack.
  • Sending transactional emails (account verification, payment confirmation, subscription expiry reminders, complaint updates).
  • Displaying vendor listings and enabling users to find and contact vendors.
  • Moderating reviews and investigating complaint submissions.
  • Security purposes: fraud detection, rate limiting, audit logging, and account protection.
  • Compliance with applicable Nigerian law, including the NDPR.

3. Data Sharing

Campuslink does not sell your personal data to any third party. We share data only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Paystack: Payment processing only. Subject to Paystack's own Privacy Policy. We share only the minimum necessary data (email, amount, reference).
  • Email Service Provider: For delivery of transactional emails only. No marketing data is shared.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose data to law enforcement or regulatory bodies when required by Nigerian law or valid legal process.
  • Business Transfer: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or asset sale, data may be transferred with prior notice to users.

4. Security Measures

We implement industry-standard and best-practice security measures to protect your data:

  • BCrypt password hashing (cost factor 12) — passwords are never stored in plaintext.
  • HTTPS/TLS encryption for all data in transit.
  • CSRF token protection on all state-changing requests.
  • Strict file upload validation with MIME type sniffing (not just extension checking).
  • PDO prepared statements to prevent SQL injection attacks.
  • Rate limiting on login and registration endpoints.
  • IP and user agent session binding to detect session hijacking.
  • Automatic session expiry after 2 hours of inactivity.
  • Comprehensive audit logging of all login attempts and admin actions.

5. Your Rights (NDPR)

Under the Nigeria Data Protection Regulation (NDPR), you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right of Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Right of Rectification: Request correction of any inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Right of Erasure: Request deletion of your personal data, subject to legal retention requirements.
  • Right to Restrict Processing: Request that we limit how we use your data in certain circumstances.
  • Right to Data Portability: Request your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
  • Right to Object: Object to processing of your data in certain circumstances.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@campuslink.ng. We will respond within 30 days.

6. Cookies

Campuslink uses only essential cookies strictly necessary for platform operation:

  • Session Cookie: Maintains your login session. HttpOnly, Secure, SameSite=Strict. Expires when you close your browser (or after 30 days if you choose "Remember Me").
  • CSRF Token: Protects against cross-site request forgery. Tied to your session.

We do not use advertising cookies, tracking cookies, analytics cookies, or any third-party cookies on our platform. You cannot opt out of essential cookies as they are required for the platform to function.

7. Data Retention

We retain personal data only as long as necessary for the purpose it was collected:

  • Active Accounts: Retained for the duration of account activity.
  • Inactive Accounts: Accounts inactive for 24+ months are subject to deletion after notice.
  • Vendor Documents: Retained for 12 months after subscription expiry for compliance purposes.
  • Payment Records: Retained for 7 years for tax and financial compliance.
  • Login Logs: Retained for 6 months for security audit purposes.
  • Complaint Records: Retained for 3 years after resolution.

8. Children's Privacy

Campuslink is not intended for use by individuals under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal data from minors. If you believe a minor has registered on our platform, please contact us immediately at privacy@campuslink.ng and we will promptly delete the account.
