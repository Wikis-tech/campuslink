Submit a Complaint

Report a vendor issue. Our admin team investigates every complaint within 48 hours.

Important Notice: Only submit genuine complaints supported by verifiable evidence. False or malicious complaints are a violation of our Terms and may result in account suspension. Three verified complaints against a vendor will trigger an admin suspension review.

Complaint Details

Enter the vendor's name exactly as listed on Campuslink.
0 / 50 characters minimum
Click to attach evidence Screenshots, receipts, chat screenshots — JPG, PNG, PDF — max 5MB each

What happens after you submit?

Within 24 hours — Acknowledgment

Your complaint is received, assigned a reference number, and assigned to an admin reviewer.

1–3 days — Investigation

Admin reviews your evidence and contacts the vendor for their response. The vendor has the right to reply.

Resolution — Admin Action

Admin takes appropriate action: formal warning, suspension, or dismissal if unfounded. You are notified by email of the outcome.

Escalation

Three verified complaints against the same vendor trigger an automatic suspension review, regardless of complaint categories.
